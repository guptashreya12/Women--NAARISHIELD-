Self Defense tips For College Students

College is an exciting and liberating time for many young adults. However, it also a very susceptible time to encounter dangerous situations as students are in a new city, with a new schedule and new friends. It does not matter what age, gender or race you are, you are vulnerable to an attack. Students who have a minimum level of self-defense training and situational awareness are more likely to avoid and be success if confronted by someone with ill-intent.
